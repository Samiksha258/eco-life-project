MIT License

## 🌍 AI-Powered Eco Activity Tracking & Gamification Platform

EcoLife Tracker is an intelligent sustainability platform that helps users track eco-friendly and eco-negative activities using AI-powered analysis, gamification, leaderboards, and environmental impact visualization.

The platform encourages users to adopt greener habits by rewarding positive environmental actions and penalizing harmful activities like pollution, deforestation, energy waste, and water wastage.

---

# 📝 Problem Statement

Environmental degradation caused by pollution, excessive energy use, water wastage, deforestation, and unsustainable daily habits has become a major global concern.

Although many people want to contribute positively toward the environment, there is a lack of interactive tools that:

- Provide instant environmental feedback
- Measure the impact of daily activities
- Encourage sustainable habits through motivation and gamification
- Make environmental awareness engaging and easy

Most existing solutions focus only on carbon footprint calculators or simple tracking systems without personalization, AI understanding, or community engagement.

EcoLife Tracker solves this problem using AI-powered natural language analysis and gamified environmental tracking.

---

# 💡 What We Built

EcoLife Tracker is an AI-powered virtual eco assistant that can:

✅ Analyze environmental activities in natural language  
✅ Reward eco-friendly actions with eco-points  
✅ Penalize harmful environmental actions  
✅ Display real-time environmental impact  
✅ Motivate users using badges, streaks, and leaderboards  
✅ Provide personalized eco suggestions  
✅ Create awareness through gamification and AI feedback
